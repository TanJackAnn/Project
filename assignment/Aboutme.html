About Me

As a pastry chef, I know that the art of baking requires continuous learning and innovation. I actively follow the latest trends and technologies in the industry, participate in baking competitions and professional training to continuously improve my skills and knowledge. I like to challenge myself to try new ingredient combinations and innovative baking methods to give customers a unique and unforgettable taste experience.

  • Passion for research:

    • I have a keen interest in baking techniques and ingredients, and enjoy delving into the properties and uses of different ingredients, as well as the chemical and physical changes that occur during the baking process. This passion drives me to seek new baking techniques and ideas.

  • Networking with peers:

    • I actively share my experience with other pastry chefs and participate in industry events and baking seminars to draw inspiration from others' experiences and make progress together.

  • Customer demand-oriented:

    • I am good at listening to customers' needs and preferences, and strive to tailor-make pastry creations for them by taking into account customers' tastes and preferences while maintaining innovation.
